About Me

Hello, my name is Renee Morrison, and I am a Registered Psychologist & Clinical Registrar located in the Hunter Valley NSW, Australia.

I highly value being able to help people improve their mental health and overall quality of life. My therapeutic style emphasises collaboration between us to create an individualised approach and achieve personally valued change.

I have varied experience working in both private practice and hospital-based settings (acute mental health). I have also facilitated a range of therapeutic group programs focusing on depression and anxiety management, addiction recovery and emotion regulation.

I am focused on nurturing a calm and open-minded atmosphere to help create a space where people feel heard, understood, and safe to share their personal experiences.

If it sounds like I might be the right person to work with you, please contact me.
